My Trips

     Pima Air Museum Tucson, Arizona: April, 2022

In April of 2022, my father and I flew over to Tucson, Arizona which is located two hour south of Phoenix and an hour north of the United States, Mexico border. One of the major parts of Tucson is Davis-Monthan Air Force base. Which runs the Airplane Boneyard that stoers the major of the United States retired Military aircaft.

History

The Air Museum was founded in 1969 by a group of aviation enthusiasts who wanted to preserve aircraft and educate the public about aviation history.Over the years, the museum has expanded its collection to include more than 400 aircraft, ranging from military fighter jets to commercial airliners. Many of these planes have played significant roles in aviation history.The museum also includes exhibits on space exploration and a restoration facility where visitors can watch planes being restored to their original condition. The Pima Air and Space Museum is a popular destination for aviation enthusiasts and tourists alike.
